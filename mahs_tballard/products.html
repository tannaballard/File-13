Athens, Greece

Welcome to the home of Plato and some of the most famous philosophers where academies where there are many Greek God and Goddess statues still there today. Imagine walking down the streets of “High City” ... close your eyes. What do you see? Centuries of ancient history. This scent takes you to warm, golden afternoon in the streets of “High City”. Featuring lemon zest, Sorrento lemons, and cane sugar.

London, England

Welcome to the city of dreams where there are astounding medieval buildings and modern structures that stand side by side and complement each other. Imagine standing in the courtyard of the Buckingham Palace… close your eyes. What do you see? Guards standing by still as a statue and people trying to distract them. This scent takes you to snowy winter day in the Buckingham Palace courtyard. Featuring lavender leaves, cool eucalyptus, and juniper berries.

Welcome to the concrete jungle where dreams and greasy pizza are made. Imagine walking in Time Square… close your eyes. What do you see? Ginormous billboards, flashy, neon lights and the hustle and bustle of New York City. This scent takes you to a cold brisk night in Time Square. Niagra Falls, Canada

Welcome to a geological wonder and home to one of the most famous waterfalls in the world where you are misted with the fresh water of Lake Erie. Imagine standing at the rails looking out to the Falls… close your eyes. What do you see? The sun reflecting through the heavy mist creating beautiful rainbows. This scent takes you to a chilly, spring day looking out to see the falls. Featuring woodland balsam, crisp eucalyptus, and fir branches
